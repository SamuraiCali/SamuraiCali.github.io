About

Golf, often associated with pristine greens and beautiful landscapes, has become increasingly aware of its environmental impact in recent years. As the sport continues to grow in popularity, golf courses around the world are taking steps to minimize their ecological footprint and embrace sustainable practices. In this blog post, we will explore the greener side of golf and how it is contributing to environmentalism.
